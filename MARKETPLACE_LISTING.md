## Full Description (Marketplace Body)

### Turn your monday.com boards into an intelligent command center

Swiftly is the AI-powered command center that goes beyond basic dashboards. It analyzes your boards in real-time, detects risks before they become problems, and lets you take action with one click — all from a single app.

**What makes Swiftly different?**

Unlike other reporting tools that just show charts, Swiftly is an intelligent agent that:
- Reads every item, subitem, status, and assignee across ALL your boards
- Automatically detects issues: stale items, missing assignees, workload imbalances, overdue tasks
- Gives you a Health Score (0-100) so you instantly know how your projects are doing
- Suggests specific actions with one-click buttons — no manual work needed

---

### Key Features

**Smart Dashboard (Command Center)**
Your boards, analyzed instantly. Get a Health Score, smart suggestions, board overview cards, and activity timeline — all auto-generated when you open the app. No buttons to click, no reports to configure.

**AI Agent**
Ask questions in plain English: "What should I prioritize this week?", "Deep dive into the Security Audit task", "Create subtasks for Database Migration". The AI references your actual items by name and suggests actions you can execute with one click.

**One-Click Actions**
When the AI suggests subtasks, click "Import" and they're created as subitems on your board instantly. Update statuses, create action plans, send reminders — all without leaving the app.

**Cross-Board Reporting**
Combine data from multiple boards into one unified report. Full subitem support, KPI dashboards, status distribution charts, and board progress comparisons. Something native monday.com dashboards can't do.

**Export Everything**
Generate professional PDF reports, plain text summaries, or JSON data exports. AI-written analysis included. Perfect for stakeholder updates, client reports, and sprint reviews.

**AI-Powered Insights**
Automatic risk detection, bottleneck identification, and actionable recommendations. Every insight comes with buttons to take action immediately.
